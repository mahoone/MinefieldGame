####Minefield 2018####

1. Use arrows to move Cat icon from the bottom to the top of the field.

2. Change difficulty level by selecting from three options; Easy, Medium and Hard.
   Each difficulty level places different amount of mines on the minefield.
   The game will restart after selecting different difficulty level.

3. Game ends if you hit the mine

4. Mines that are nearest are counted and shown at the top e.g. if 2 mines are nearby then display will show '2'.
